Powerful Learning Tools

Each visualizer is engineered to show not just WHAT happens — but WHY it happens at every step.

Binary Search Tree

Watch BST insertion, deletion, and traversal algorithms unfold in real-time. Understand rotations, balancing, and height optimization with annotated visuals.

Red-Black Tree Simulator

See color-flips and rotations animate live. Understand every rebalancing rule: uncle red? Rotate. Uncle black? Double rotate. Explained step-by-step.

B+ Tree Builder

Construct multi-level B+ trees and watch page splits cascade. Visualize how databases index your data with minimum reads guaranteed.
